Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your trip.

We recommend booking at least 4–6 weeks in advance for domestic tours and 8–12 weeks for international packages. This ensures the best availability and pricing for flights and hotels. During peak seasons (October–January), book even earlier.

Every package clearly lists what's included. Typically, this covers return flights, hotel accommodation, daily breakfast, airport/hotel transfers, a local English-speaking guide, and entry tickets to major attractions. We always show a detailed inclusions list before you confirm booking.

Cancellations made 30+ days before departure receive a full refund minus processing fees. Cancellations 15–29 days before incur a 25% charge. Less than 15 days: 50% charge. We strongly recommend purchasing our travel insurance, which covers unforeseen cancellations.

Yes! Our team provides full visa guidance for all destinations we offer. We help you prepare the correct documents, fill out application forms, and submit to the right embassy or VFS centre. While we cannot guarantee visa approval, our documentation support makes the process smooth and stress-free.

Absolutely! All our packages can be tailored to your specific needs — whether that means changing hotels, adding extra nights, including specific activities, or adjusting meal plans. Contact us and a travel specialist will design a custom itinerary just for you, free of charge.

Travel insurance is not mandatory for domestic tours but is strongly recommended for all international packages. We offer comprehensive travel insurance plans that cover medical emergencies, trip cancellations, lost baggage, and flight delays at competitive rates.
