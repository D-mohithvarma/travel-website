Who We Are

Passionate Travellers Who Became Your Travel Experts

Founded in 2005 by a group of globe-trotting friends who believed that travel should be accessible, effortless, and truly transformative, Wanderlust Travel has grown into one of India's most trusted travel agencies.

We don't just book flights and hotels. We design complete experiences — immersing you in local culture, connecting you with authentic communities, and creating memories that last a lifetime.

From budget backpacker trips to luxury honeymoon escapes, we have curated over 200 unique packages across 80+ destinations worldwide.

  • IATA certified and government recognized travel agency
  • 15,000+ happy customers across 10 years
  • 24/7 on-trip support and emergency assistance
  • Transparent pricing — no hidden charges ever
  • Sustainable and responsible tourism practices
